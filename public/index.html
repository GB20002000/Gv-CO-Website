About Us

G.V & Co is a leading construction company with over 25 years of excellence. We specialize in on-site building construction and tar road development, complete with electrical installations, CCTV systems, and other essential infrastructure. We use high-quality materials, modern machinery, and well-planned methods for strong foundations, durable structures, and efficient road networks.
